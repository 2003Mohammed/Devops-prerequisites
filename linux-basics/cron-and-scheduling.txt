# Cron Jobs and Scheduling

Cron automates repetitive tasks.

## Crontab
- crontab -l
- crontab -e

## Format
* * * * * command
(min hour day month weekday)

Example:
0 2 * * * backup.sh

## System Cron
- /etc/crontab
- /etc/cron.d/

## DevOps Use Case
Log cleanup, backups, health checks.
