# Logs and Monitoring in Linux

Logs are the first place to look when something breaks.

## System Logs
- /var/log/messages
- /var/log/syslog
- /var/log/auth.log

## View Logs
- cat file.log
- less file.log
- tail -f file.log

## journalctl
- journalctl
- journalctl -u service_name
- journalctl --since "1 hour ago"

## DevOps Use Case
Essential for debugging failures in CI/CD pipelines, servers, containers, and services.
