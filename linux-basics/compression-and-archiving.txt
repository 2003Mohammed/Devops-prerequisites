# Compression and Archiving

Used for backups, artifacts, and log rotation.

## tar
- tar -cvf files.tar file1 file2
- tar -xvf files.tar

## gzip / gunzip
- gzip file.txt
- gunzip file.txt.gz

## zip
- zip app.zip app/
- unzip app.zip

## DevOps Use Case
Used in CI pipelines, backups, artifact storage.
